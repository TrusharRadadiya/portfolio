About

Game Developer & Designer

Hi there! I am Trushar Radadiya, and I am a game developer & game designer with over 2 years of experience in this industry. I have a Bachelor's degree in Computer Application from Veer Narmad South Gujarat University.

I have worked on a variety of projects ranging from mobile games to AR/VR projects and many more. I am skilled in Unity Game Engine, C# programming language, Audacity for sound designing, Adobe Photoshop & Illustrator for UI/UX & graphics, and VEED.io & Canva for video editing. Currently, I am working on my video editing skills with Adobe Premiere Pro.

In my free time, I enjoy staying up-to-date with the latest tech trends and working on personal games. I also like to brainstorm new game mechanics and game ideas.

I am passionate about using my skills and knowledge to create innovative and engaging games, and am always looking for new opportunities to learn and grow as a developer.

When any client hires me my only goal is to solve their problem with the best practical solution and delivering the best quality product.
